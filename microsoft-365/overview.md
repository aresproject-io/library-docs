# Overview

## What is Microsoft 365

Microsoft 365 is a cloud-based subscription service that provides a suite of productivity tools and services for businesses and individuals. It includes the latest versions of the Microsoft Office suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook, and OneNote), as well as cloud-based versions of Microsoft's next-generation communications and collaboration services, such as Microsoft Teams, SharePoint Online, and Exchange Online.

### Commercial

This is the standard Microsoft 365 cloud that is used by most businesses and individuals.

### Government Community Cloud (GCC)

This cloud is designed for US government agencies and contractors. It is hosted in Microsoft's US-based data centers and meets the specific compliance requirements of the US government.

### Government Community Cloud High (GCC-H)

This cloud is designed for US government agencies and contractors that specific levels of unclassified data, typically referred to as Controllable Unclassified Information (CUI). It is hosted in Microsoft's US-based data centers and meets specific compliance requirements.
